About Me

Greetings! My name is Aaron Dunn and I am the Product Manager for Eccovia Solutions in Salt Lake City. I bring a high level of passion to any project I work on as well as a desire to learn. Through the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp, I have gained further knowledge of many different languages, which I have applied to my personal and professional life.

I work well with others and often find myself in leadership positions. I love using my skills and knowledge to help others, which is why I have worked for 5+ years in the social services field. I also own a small technical consultation business: Mimir Consulting. I am organized and have a wide ability to multitask.
